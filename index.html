About M Tech Textile Traders

Who We Are

Working since 1980, M Tech Textile Traders is a trusted expert in spinning machinery—especially Open-End RN, Autocoro, Ring, and Autoconer systems. Since 2020, we’ve been importing high-quality machinery from China, the USA, and Tajikistan. With decades of hands-on experience, we believe our true earning is customer trust and satisfaction.

Our Vision

Our goal is to become a global leader in textile machinery by focusing on customer satisfaction, quality service, and long-term relationships. We believe that earning trust is more valuable than earning profit. Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to deliver solutions that meet both immediate and future needs. At M Tech, we build partnerships—not just transactions—because we believe in growing together with our clients.

Spinning Machinery Solutions
